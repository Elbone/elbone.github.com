I’m an Australian designer and artist. Born in Germany, raised in Tasmania, with most of my adult life spent in Melbourne, with a few globe-spanning side trips along the way. I recently returned to Tasmania with my wife, son, and dog.

On one european adventure at 19 I was introduced to {% include link.html href="https://www.instagram.com/nielsoeltjen/" text="graffiti lyfe" %}, and I haven’t been able to shake the addiction since. I make {% include link.html href="http://www.nails.net.au/" text="art" %} across many media.

My working life has been varied and rich, from the early days working in small culturally-leaning {% include link.html href="http://www.letterbox.net.au/" text="design studios" %}, through top of town advertising, and software and startups. I haven’t been content to stand still, and I don’t think I’ll ever stop looking for new challenges in this thing called ‘design’. The last five years have been focused very much on startup and tech land, and I’ve felt right at home.

Many creative pursuits occupy my waking life — lettering, graffiti, drawing, gardening, making home wares, home improvement, and of course my work as a Digital Maker.

Dodging {% include link.html href="https://www.instagram.com/p/9z377HnUmO/?taken-by=nielsoeltjen" text="Blue-tongue Lizards" %} and Wallabies while trail running with my dog, Nutmeg, is my on-the-reg workout.
